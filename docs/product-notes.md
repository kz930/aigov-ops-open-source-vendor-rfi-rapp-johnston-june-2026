# Safe-RFI Product Notes

## What Safe-RFI is

Safe-RFI is an AIGovOps Foundation open-source experiment for vendor RFI workflows in AI governance programs.

It is optimized for evidence-oriented comparison and transparent tradeoffs across capability domains.

## Workflow alignment

- **Beacon alignment**: receipt-friendly decision output and unsigned preview patterns.
- **Umbrella alignment**: policy and controls framing for deployment governance.
- **Lantern path**: future diff-friendly content pathway for change comparison across runs.

## Design intent

- Static and portable
- Deterministic output model
- Auditor-oriented narrative support
- Neutral sponsorship governance
